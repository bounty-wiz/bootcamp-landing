Curriculum overview

Exact topics may be adjusted based on the cohort’s level, but the structure will look like this:

Week 1 – Ethereum & node internals

  • How Ethereum clients actually work (Geth, Erigon, Nethermind)
  • Transactions, mempools, and state transitions in detail
  • Hands-on: run a local node, inspect blocks & traces

Week 2 – Smart contract execution & security

  • EVM execution model and gas
  • Common pitfalls & real-world vulnerabilities
  • Hands-on: write, test, and break contracts with Foundry

Week 3 – DeFi, DEXs, and aggregators

  • How AMMs & DEXs actually work under the hood
  • Building a minimal Uniswap-style swap flow
  • Hands-on: implement a simple price routing / aggregation logic

Week 4 – Cross-chain & infrastructure

  • RPC providers, reliability, and monitoring
  • Cross-chain messaging and bridging patterns
  • Hands-on: build infra scripts in Go / TS

Week 5 – Production patterns

  • Signature verification & EIP-712
  • Batching, multicalls, and gas optimization
  • Hands-on: production-ready deployment pipeline

Week 6 – Capstone project

  • Design & build a real-world dApp or protocol component
  • Code review, architecture feedback, and deployment
  • Final presentation + next-steps roadmap
